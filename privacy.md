# Privacy Policy

This application is a **demo version of a B2B mobile app for OroCommerce**.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data Collection

The app does **not collect, store, or process personal data on its own servers**.  
All data displayed in the app is retrieved directly from the connected **OroCommerce backend**, which is fully controlled by the business using the application.

The app may process the following data **only to provide core functionality**:

- user account information provided by the OroCommerce server
- product, pricing, and order data
- authentication tokens (OAuth 2.0)

Authentication tokens are stored securely on the device and used only for communication with the configured OroCommerce API.

## Data Sharing

The app does **not share user data with third parties**.  
No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services are used.
